Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

HookahMap ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Location data — used only to show nearby hookah lounges. Never stored on our servers.
  • Account information — email address if you create an account (optional).
  • Usage data — anonymized analytics to improve app performance (e.g., crash reports).
  • User content — reviews, ratings, and venue suggestions you voluntarily submit.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display relevant hookah venues near your location.
  • To save your favorites and preferences across sessions.
  • To improve app features and fix bugs.
  • To respond to your support requests.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share anonymized, aggregated data with third-party analytics providers. Location data is processed on-device and is never transmitted to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

HookahMap uses the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — for in-app advertising
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — for app distribution
  • Supabase — for backend data storage (venue database)

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

HookahMap is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
