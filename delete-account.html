Delete Your Account

To delete your HookahMap account and all associated data, follow the steps below inside the app.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the HookahMap app
  2. Go to Profile tab
  3. Tap Edit Profile
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion
⚠️ Account deletion is permanent. Your profile, reviews, and all personal data will be removed and cannot be recovered.
